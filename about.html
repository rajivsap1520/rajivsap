About Our Company

Built for small & medium businesses in India. We're on a mission to make business management simple, accessible, and affordable for everyone.

Our Story

Empowering Small Businesses Across India

BizManager was born from a simple observation: small business owners in India spend countless hours managing paper registers, struggling with calculations, and losing track of important business data.

We recognized that traditional business management tools were either too complex, too expensive, or required constant internet connectivity—none of which worked well for Indian small businesses.

That's why we created BizManager—an offline-first, easy-to-use mobile app that brings professional business management capabilities to every kirana store, mobile shop, hardware store, and small business owner across India.

Our Mission

To empower every small and medium business in India with professional, affordable, and easy-to-use tools that help them grow, compete, and succeed in the digital age—without needing constant internet connectivity or technical expertise.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted business management platform for small businesses, helping millions of entrepreneurs save time, reduce errors, and make data-driven decisions that accelerate their business growth.
