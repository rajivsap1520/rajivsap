Privacy Policy

Your data safety is our priority. Learn how we protect your information.

Last Updated: January 18, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to BizManager ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your business data. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our business management mobile application ("the App").

Key Principle: BizManager is designed as an offline-first application. Your business data is stored locally on your device, and we do not collect, transmit, or store your business information on our servers.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

When you use BizManager, you may input various types of business information directly into the App, including:

  • Business name and details
  • Product and inventory information
  • Customer names and contact details
  • Invoice and transaction records
  • Financial data (sales, purchases, payments)

Important: All this information is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to, collect, or store this data on our servers.

3. How We Use Information

3.1 Local Data Storage

All your business data entered into BizManager is:

  • Stored exclusively on your device's local storage
  • Used solely to provide App functionality to you
  • Never transmitted to our servers or any third party
  • Accessible only to you through the App

4. Data Security

We take data security seriously and have implemented appropriate measures to protect your information:

4.1 Local Storage Security

  • Your data is stored in a secure local database on your device
  • We use industry-standard encryption methods where applicable
  • Access to your data is protected by your device's security features

4.2 Your Responsibility

Since all data is stored locally, you are responsible for:

  • Securing your device with a strong password/PIN/biometric lock
  • Protecting your device from unauthorized access, loss, or theft
  • Creating regular backups of your important data
  • Keeping your device's operating system and security patches up to date

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

5.1 We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your business information to anyone, ever. Your data is yours, and yours alone.

6. Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You can access all your data through the App at any time
  • Correction: You can modify or update your data through the App's editing features
  • Deletion: You can delete your data at any time
  • Export: You can export your data in common formats (PDF, Excel, etc.)
  • Control: You have complete control over your data since it's stored locally

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@bizmanager.com
  • Phone: +91 98765 43210
  • WhatsApp: +91 98765 43210

Our Privacy Commitment

Your privacy is our priority. BizManager is built with privacy by design. Your business data stays on your device, under your control. We will never sell your data or use it for advertising. You own your data, and we respect that.
